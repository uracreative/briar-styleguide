Briar Style Guide

Resources, components, and design guidelines behind Briar

About

Briar is a messaging app designed for activists, journalists, and anyone else who needs a safe, easy and robust way to communicate. Unlike traditional messaging apps, Briar doesn't rely on a central server - messages are synchronized directly between the users' devices.

This guide aims to provide standards, principles and components to design consistent views related to the Briar Project. These pages are a living document and we welcome contributions, feedback and suggestions.
